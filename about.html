ABOUT

Hi! My name is Ellie Hollenberger and I am a third year student at the University of Minnesota Studying Computer Science as well as graphic and web Design.

Photography started as a hobby for me when I was in high school, I was gifted a camera for Christmas by my wonderful parents, and ever since have been taking pictures of whatever I can. Mu close friends, family on holidays, and high school teamates. The only thing that is rare to catch in front of the lense is me!

During my time at Menomonee Falls High School, I took several art and photography classes ending with AP Studio Art, Photo. I created two portfolio for this class, both of which I am still very proud of. They are titled “Doodles” and “Distortion” and are featured on the portfolio page.

I have always loved taking pictures of people, my friends, family, but especially kids. I had the opportunity to volunteer at a nutrition house in Panama with kids 0-3, and taking their portraits is where that love began. Since I have enjoyed taking pictures of kids all ages, but especially babies.
